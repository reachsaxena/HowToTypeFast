Typeing.com Typeing Club How To Type

Learn to type the U and R key

For the R key you use your left index finger. For the Ukey use your right index finger like the H and J key.

How to practic and get faster

To practic you should try to make fake words up and try to type them into the searchbar and try not to make a any mistacks.A tip to get faster at typeing you should try not to look at the keyboard and look at your screen.This might take a while but soon you will get used to it.

Learn to type the I and E key

To type the I key you need to use your right middle key finger.For the E key you also need your middle finger but your left one.

Learn to type the O and W key

For the O key you need to use your right ring finger. For the W key you also use the ring finger on your left hand.

Quick tip to learn faster

To help you learn faster you should not learn the hole hoem row in one day.You should split it in the 15 minutes each day and this will help because if you learn it in one day your hands will start hurrting and that will slow things down.

Learn the P and Q key

For the P and Q you use your left and right pinky.
